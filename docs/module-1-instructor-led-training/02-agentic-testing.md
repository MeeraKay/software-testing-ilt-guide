# 2. Agentic Testing: From Requirement to Test Cases

## What is agentic testing?

UiPath is moving towards an agentic future, with a focus on agentic testing for software testing teams.

**Agentic Testing** means augmenting testers with AI agents to extend, accelerate, and simplify their work — ultimately helping them be more productive and find greater joy in testing.

Agentic testing with UiPath Test Cloud provides you with:

- A broad range of out-of-the-box AI capabilities to boost your testing throughout the entire testing lifecycle. **Autopilot for Testers** is a collection of AI-powered digital systems — agents — designed to boost tester productivity.
- All the tools you need to build custom AI agents tailored to your unique testing needs, giving you the flexibility to build exactly what you need, when you need it, your way.

Agents represent the next generation of automation. They are well suited for non-deterministic, dynamic use cases, and leverage large language models (LLMs) to plan, think, take action, and learn over time.

## 1. Out-of-the-box agents — Autopilot for Testers

Autopilot for Testers is a collection of AI-powered digital systems (agents) designed to boost the productivity of testers throughout the entire testing lifecycle.

These capabilities are integrated into UiPath Studio Desktop and UiPath Test Manager.
